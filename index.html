Our Story

Design-Forward Landscaping, Rooted in Barrie

Atrium Landscapes was born from a belief that outdoor spaces deserve the same architectural consideration as interiors. Based in Barrie, Ontario, we bring a design-first philosophy to every project — whether it's a sprawling estate garden or an intimate urban courtyard.

Our team combines formal training in landscape architecture with decades of hands-on horticultural and construction experience. We don't just plant gardens; we compose outdoor rooms, sculpt landforms, and craft settings where memories take root.

Every project begins with listening — to our clients' aspirations, to the site's natural character, and to the architectural language of the home. The result is landscapes that feel inevitable, as though they've always belonged.
